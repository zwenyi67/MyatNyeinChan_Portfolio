Shan Yoe Ma Commercial Hub

Tachileik

Existing Shan Yoe Ma Department Store is old, damaged, and not in operational state. After surveying the city’s economy, lifestyle, and market demand, we decided to propose a new marketplace rather than preservation or refurbishment.

Planning includes a supermarket and shophouses on a 3.3-acre site in the heart of Tachileik, integrated with various commercial and recreational facilities such as a movie theatre, spa, and children’s playground.
