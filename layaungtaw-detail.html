La Yaung Taw ( Main Building)

Nay Pyi Taw

This project seeks to create a space of residential building with the sensation of forest retreat, creating architecture that interacts with vegetation and climate.

Designing a modern residential which reflecting the whole master plan and enrich with tradional architecture concept as the build has a plan to accomodate as memorial museum of Daw Aung San Su Kyi in future.

Sitting at the hill top of Ottara Thiri Township, we carefully design to blend with surrounding topography and vegetation. Triangle is the strongest in geometry shapes. Ancient Burmese used triangle concept to get harmony and supermacy with the enviroment as the stupas were built at the top of hills. We use the concept at the front wall where visitors might see the building first, reminding the location of site, the top of the hill. The crescent moon shape lobby reflect the name of the project, La Yaung Taw (Moonlight Forest)whilst expressing grand, elegant and Simplicity.
